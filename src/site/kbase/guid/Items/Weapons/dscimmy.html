Dragon Scimitar

Item Id : 4587

The dragon scimmy is a level 60 attack weapon forged by the great Apes of Atoll! The weapon usually requires the completion of Monkey Madness; however, in 2009sacpe since it's not implemented yet you will only be required to have a magic level of 64 to use the ape atoll teleport. You could have a buddy of yours teleport for you if you do not have the required level. This will be changed once the quest is implemented.

Shop : It may be purchased from Daga on Ape Atoll for 100,000 coins

Attack Bonus +8 attack stab + 67 attack slash -2 attack crush +0 attack magic +0 attack ranged
Defence Bonus +0 defence stab +1 defence slash +0 defence crush +0 defence magic +0 defence ranged
Other Bonuses +66 Strength +0 Ranged Strength +0 Magic Strength +0 prayer
